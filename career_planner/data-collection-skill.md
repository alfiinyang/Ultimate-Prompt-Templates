—
name: data-collection
description: Ask user structured and sequential questions based on resource documents to understand the user's current level and career aspirations.
—

## Purpose
Present user with questions from the Questions section below in their specified order.
Explain ambiguous questions when asked (“Can you clarify what you mean by…?”). Provided examples where necessary or if hinted.
Maintain a dynamic memory of user responses.

## Output Data
user_profile: everything about the user’s values as captured in SECTION 1, in brief bullets
skill_inventory: current technical and soft skills
direction: career direction as indicated by user

## Questions

### **Section 1 – Start with your values**

1. What motivates me and what do I enjoy doing?
2. What are my personal attributes and lifestyle priorities?
3. What do my family and friends see as my strengths and weaknesses?
4. What are the five important factors or non-negotiables I am looking for in a job?
5. Do I prefer leading a group, working with peers or working alone?
6. What energizes me? What drains my energy?
7. Do I have a specific calling in life?
8. What about my career motivates me to get up and go to work in the morning?

👉 You can answer them one by one or as a flowing response that covers them together.

---

### **Section 2 – Evaluate Your Skills**

1. What qualifications and experience do I have?
2. What are my key strengths, transferable skills and technical skills?
3. What are my biggest achievements to date?
4. What are my weaknesses and areas for development?

👉 You can answer them one by one or as a flowing response that covers them together.

---

### **Section 3 – Career Direction**

**Q1. Future Work Vision**
* What kind of work do you want to be doing in 5–10 years?
* Do you want to stay in technical roles, move into leadership, or combine both?

**Q2. Industry & Impact**
* Which industry do you want to make an impact in (e.g., AI research, education, telecom, tech entrepreneurship)?
* What problems do you want your work to help solve (e.g., African prosperity, low-resource language education, ethical AI)?

**Q3. Work Environment**
* Do you see yourself in academia/research, private sector, startup/entrepreneurship, or a hybrid of these?
* What type of organizations would you like to work with (big tech, research labs, universities, your own company, NGOs)?

**Q4. Legacy & Recognition**
* When people look back at your career, what do you want to be known for?
* What impact do you want to leave on your community, Nigeria, and Africa at large?

👉 You can answer them one by one or as a flowing response that covers them together.
